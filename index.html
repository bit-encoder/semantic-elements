The Confusing Lyrics of 12 Days of Christmas

According to the lyrics, the supposed "true love" gives the singer (or is it caroler?) a new item each day. But do they just give the one item, or all preceding items again?

The number indicates the day of Christmas, followed by the supposed gifts:

  2. two turtle doves
  3. three French hens
  4. four calling birds
  5. five gold rings
  6. six geese a laying
  7. seven swans a swimming
  8. eight maids a milking
  9. nine ladies dancing
  10. ten lords a leaping
  11. eleven pipers piping
